What each check does:

Known word pairs — unit-test style: confirms the lemmatizer handles specific inflected forms correctly, including the POS-dependent case (leaves → leaf vs. leave).
Suspicious suffixes — scans the full vocabulary for frequent tokens still carrying -ing, -ies, -ations that should have been reduced.
Word family collapse — checks whether variant forms (e.g. feminists / feminist) now share a single entry, which is the whole point of lemmatization.
Random row sample — a quick eyeball check on real data to catch anything unexpected.
